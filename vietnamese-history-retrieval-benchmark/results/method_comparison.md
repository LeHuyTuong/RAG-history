# Method Comparison

This is a preliminary evaluation on a silver-label pilot benchmark. The qrels were generated by an LLM-assisted labeling process and are not manually verified labels.

## Metrics

| method    |   num_queries |   recall_at_5 |   recall_at_10 |   mrr_at_10 |   evidence_hit_rate_at_5 |   citation_accuracy_at_5 |   no_answer_accuracy |
|:----------|--------------:|--------------:|---------------:|------------:|-------------------------:|-------------------------:|---------------------:|
| bm25      |            40 |        0.6842 |         0.7368 |      0.5877 |                   0.6842 |                   0.6842 |                    0 |
| e5        |            40 |        0.1579 |         0.2368 |      0.1118 |                   0.1579 |                   0.1842 |                    0 |
| hybrid_e5 |            40 |        0.6053 |         0.7105 |      0.4119 |                   0.6053 |                   0.6053 |                    0 |

## Best Method

bm25 achieved the best aggregate primary score on the silver-label pilot set.

Aggregate primary score used for this summary: `0.6696` over Recall@5, Recall@10, and MRR@10.

Do not generalize this result beyond the local 15-PDF corpus.
